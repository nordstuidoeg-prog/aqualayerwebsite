About AquaLayer

A protection authority, not a cleaner.

AquaLayer was built around a single shift in thinking: a premium vessel doesn’t need cleaning — it needs protecting. Egypt’s sun and salt age a hull every day it sits unprotected. We exist to put a deliberate, engineered layer between your luxury and the sea.

What drives us

Mission, vision, purpose.

Mission

Protect what owners love.

To give every vessel in Egypt access to genuine marine-grade surface protection, applied to a specialist standard.

Vision

Set the category.

To be the reference for marine protection in the region — the name marinas, brokers and owners trust first.

Purpose

Preserved luxury.

To keep every vessel looking newer, longer — so its beauty and its value last well beyond the next season.

02

Built for Egypt

Engineered for the conditions that age a hull.

Year-round UV. Warm, saline water. Long sun-exposed berths from the Red Sea to the Mediterranean. These are the conditions that oxidize gelcoat, fade interiors and corrode metals faster than most owners expect.

  • Red Sea — relentless UV and salt; lead with longevity, PPF and oxidation defense.
  • North Coast & Alexandria — seasonal use and storage; pre-season correction and protection.

The difference

Marine-native, not automotive.

Most coating work in the region is adapted from cars. The sea is a harder problem — different surfaces, different chemistry, different failure modes. AquaLayer is built marine-first: the products, the process and the expectations are all matched to vessels.

That is the difference between a service and an authority. A service gives your boat a wash. An authority reads the surface, protects it correctly, and tells you the truth about what it will hold.
