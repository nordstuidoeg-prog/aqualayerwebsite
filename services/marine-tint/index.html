What it is

Engineered protection, not a quick shine.

Marine tint is a specialist film for helm and cabin glass. It reduces solar heat and glare, blocks the UV that fades interiors, and adds privacy — tuned for marine glass and the salt environment.

It is not automotive tint. The film, the shades and the installation are all chosen for the realities of a vessel exposed to sun and sea.
