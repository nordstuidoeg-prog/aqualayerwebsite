What it is

Engineered protection, not a quick shine.

Detailing is how a protected vessel stays protected. Our packages map to oxidation level — maintain a sound surface, restore a tired one, or fully reset a neglected one — always finishing with a protective layer.

We come to your berth, work to a marine-grade standard, and recommend only the package your vessel actually needs.

How it works

The process.

01

Inspect & map oxidation

We read the surface and grade its condition from L1 to L3.

02

Choose the package

Wash & Wax, Polish & Protect, or the 15-stage AquaLayer Package — matched to condition.

03

Execute

Wash and decontaminate, correct as needed, and apply protection.

04

Protect & guide

Finish with a protective layer and a simple maintenance plan.

Lifespan & expectations

What to expect.

3–9Months · detailing protection

Detailing protection holds for 3–9 months depending on package, use and conditions. We will advise an honest interval for how your vessel is berthed and run.

Packages

Three packages, mapped to condition.

Each package maps to an oxidation level — and finishes with protection.

No / light oxidation · L1

Wash & Wax

Premium maintenance for a sound surface — kept clean, protected and gleaming between deeper services.

  • Hand wash & salt decontamination
  • Gentle clay/decon as needed
  • Protective wax or sealant
  • Glass, rails and fittings wipe-down
Light–medium oxidation · L2

Polish & Protect

Restoration before protection — light correction lifts oxidation and brings gloss back, then a protective layer locks it in.

  • Everything in Wash & Wax
  • Single/two-stage machine polish
  • Oxidation & water-spot reduction
  • Sealant or ceramic-ready finish
Medium–heavy oxidation · L3

AquaLayer Package

A 15-stage full restoration and protection prep for neglected gelcoat — the complete reset before ceramic or PPF.

  • 15-stage restoration process
  • Multi-stage gelcoat correction
  • Full decontamination & prep
  • Protection-ready, corrected surface
